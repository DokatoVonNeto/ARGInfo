Codes & ciphers

There are many different types of Codes and Ciphers to use some of them can be easily cracked others require years of research. This page will teach you some of the most basic forms and how to crack them.

Reccomended

By click the word “Recommended” you will be transferred to a website that has basic ciphers and codes easily accessible to the world. We will be using some of the most easily identifiable ciphers. These being the ciphers by the name of the following: Keyword Cipher, Morse code, Caesar cipher/ Caesarian shift, ROT13, and base 32/64, and binary code.

The examples all show how “the moon is dark” is translated in each cipher.

Using a keyword, you can decrypt the text.

Morse code:

This old code is still used, today as a method of communication even between languages. It transfers all letters into a series of dots and dashes.

- .... . / -- --- --- -. / .. ... / -.. .- .-. -.-
